Welcome to NeighborNet, your go-to platform for staying connected with your community. Our web application allows you to engage with your neighbors, share updates, and stay informed about what’s happening around you. Whether you’re interested in local events, neighborhood discussions, or just want to connect with fellow residents, NeighborNet provides a convenient space to keep you in the loop.

With NeighborNet, you can easily create an account and start participating in your neighborhood’s digital community. Publish posts, organize events, and share important updates with your neighbors. Whether you’re announcing a yard sale, looking for recommendations, or planning a community gathering, our platform is designed to facilitate meaningful connections and open communication.

One of the key features of NeighborNet is our integrated Google Maps overlay. This allows you to visually explore local events happening around your area, making it easy to find and join activities that matter to you. Stay connected, stay informed, and become an active part of your neighborhood with NeighborNet.
